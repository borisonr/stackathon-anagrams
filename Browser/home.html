Welcome to Anagrams

Rules of the Game

  • Player 1 flips the first tile, and then everyone take turns flipping one at a time
  • If you see a word that can be made from the main pile, type it in and submit it
  • Words must be at least 3 letters long and be found in the English dictionary
  • You can also steal a word from another player by adding a letter to the word from the main pile, but you have to change the meaning of the word (just adding an "s" doesn't count)
